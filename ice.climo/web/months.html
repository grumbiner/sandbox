This page makes use of JavaScript to permit display of interatively selected months of ice cover. Users should note that the data themselves are available from the National Snow and Ice Data Center, as is an important document describing the details of how the grids were derived. One important feature is that these fields are not averages in any sense. They are representations of the sea ice cover at the end of the named months.
